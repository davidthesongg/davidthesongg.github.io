3 D

For this project, I printed out my Minecraft Among-Us statue using a 3D printer. My professor printed out the smaller model for me, which took about 4 hours to print. I printed out the larger model, which took about 16-17 hours to print. After printing it out, I spent A LOT of time removing the supports that were also printed. I learned the hard way to make a model that doesn't require too many supports.
