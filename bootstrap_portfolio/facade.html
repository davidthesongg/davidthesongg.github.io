Facade

This is my final project for Art 74. For this project, I made a .gif that symbolizes the facade that many seemingly perfect and happy people put on for the public. I wanted to emphasize how everyone has stressful things going on in their lives and nobody's life is perfect. Even though someone may physically appear normal/stable, only they can tell what's going on inside their head. Whether it be academic or peer pressure, parents fighting, significant loss, heartbreak, etc... everyone goes through something in life. Ultimately, the most important thing is to be there for each other and to persevere through those hard times.
