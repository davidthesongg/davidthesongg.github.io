HISTORY OF CATS

by David Song

Cats, beloved by many and feared by some, have a long and complex history with humans. These furry creatures have been a part of our lives for thousands of years, serving as companions, protectors of crops, and even as sacred animals in some cultures. However, despite their close relationship with humans, cats have also endured immense suffering throughout history.

From being persecuted as demonic creatures during the Middle Ages to being hunted for their fur and meat in some parts of the world today, cats have often been victims of human cruelty and neglect.

Despite their suffering, cats continue to captivate and inspire us with their grace, independence, and intelligence. This website will explore the fascinating history of the domestication of cats.
